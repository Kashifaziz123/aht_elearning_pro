Functionality and settings to Sign in & Register

  • Go to home page, click on register.


  • Enter your details, and register yourself.


  • After registration you can now sign in by clicking on the login button.


Functionality and settings to sign up as a Instructor

  • Go to home page, click on become a instructor.


  • Click on get started


  • Enter sign up details


Functionality and settings to send course for Approval.

  • Once the course is created it's in the draft state, and you got a option for send approval request. "Click on it"


  • Once clicked the state will be changed to "Pending Approval"


  • Now only "Admin" can approve the courses. All unapproved courses will be available in unapproved courses.


  • Select the course from the list below for approval.


  • Now here you can approve or reject the course.


  • Once accepted the state will be changed to "Approved", and Publish button will be available to publish the course. Courses can be published by instructors as well after approval.


  • Once Published, you now have option to unpublish the course and visit the course on the website.


Functionality and settings for previewing course video

  • Enter video link in "Preview URL" and go to website.


  • Here you can preivew your course video.
